MediMeet-Healthcare-App
Project Overview:
MediMeet is a human-centered healthcare mobile application designed to improve emergency response and healthcare accessibility for elderly and rural users.
The app integrates emergency ambulance booking, AI chatbot support, video consultation, medicine reminders, and digital health records into one seamless experience.

Problem Statement:
Many elderly and rural users struggle to access healthcare services due to:
   Long travel distances
   Delayed emergency response
   Lack of proper guidance
   Complex healthcare apps
Existing platforms mainly focus on doctor consultation but lack integrated emergency and accessibility support.

Solution:
MediMeet provides:
   Emergency SOS ambulance booking
   Real-time ambulance tracking
   Video consultation
   AI health chatbot
   Medicine reminders
   Digital health records
   Smart notifications

Elderly Patients
